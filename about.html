About Me

I am a graduate student at Dedan Kimathi University pursuing Masters of Science in Telecommunication Engineering. I am also a research intern at the Centre for Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAIL) in Dedan Kimathi University of Technology. I have experience in Digital Signal Processing, Bioacoustics, programming in Python, Machine Learning and Data Science and fabricating systems for data collection for machine learning purposes. I am driven by the passion to develop solutions to the problems we face in our every day lives using data science. I am especially interested in using data science and machine learning in studying the ecosystem with the intention of conserving it. Currently, I am working on a project to classify birds automatically based on their vocalization. I have developed an acoustic system that is able to detect acoustic activties of birds and save their vocalizations. I intend to use the collected audio data to develop machine learning models to automatically classify birds for the purpose of studying the ecosystem.
